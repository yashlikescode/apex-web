About Us

Engineering Tomorrow's
Power Solutions Today

Established in 1997 and headquartered in Jamshedpur, Jharkhand, Apex Marketing has emerged as a leading manufacturer of high-quality electrical control panels and switchgear systems.

We specialise in the design, manufacturing, and supply of electrical panels, switchgear, and automation solutions. With a strong foundation built on quality, reliability, and long-term customer relationships, Apex Marketing continues to deliver efficient, performance-driven solutions tailored to the evolving needs of modern industries.
